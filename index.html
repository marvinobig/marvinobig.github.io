About Me

The summer was about to end and I was heading into my third year at university. I was just googling around and I came across freeCodeCamp. That was when my journey into development really began. It was through freeCodeCamp that I learnt HTML & CSS. University taught me JavaScript but The Odin Project taught me to understand it, taught me to enjoy it and then taught me how to really learn. After my first experience with coding, I realised that this is something I enjoyed a lot, being able to have a mixture of creativity and logic and teach myself how to turn an idea into a product/service or develop someone's ideas into reality.
