Reuben Sandwich

Description

Prep: 15 mins, Cook: 10 mins.
Total: 25 mins


Macros

  • Protein: 31.2g
  • Carbohydrates: 43.5g
  • Fat: 40.3g

Micros

  • Cholesterol: 115mg
  • Sodium: 1930.3mg.

Per Serving: 657 calories


Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter

  • 8 slices rye bread

  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef

  • 8 slices Swiss cheese

  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained

  • Â½ cup Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle on medium heat.

  2. Lightly butter one side of bread slices. Spread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing.

  3. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and second slice of Swiss cheese.

  4. Top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides out.

  5. Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
