THE REFLECTIVE PRACTITIONER.

WITH A CHOSEN PRINCIPLE OF SYMETRY AND REPETITION, I CREATED A VISUAL NARRATIVE OF MY HANDS.

MY STORY-
HANDS. WOMEN'S HANDS. OH, HOW DELICATE THEY MUST BE. OH, HOW BEAUTIFUL THEY MUST BE. THEY SHOULD BE TENDER AND SMOOTH. THEY SHOULD SHOW LOVE AND AFFECTION. BUT WHAT IF MY HANDS AREN'T BEAUTIFUL AS SWEET FLOWERS? WHAT IF THEY ARE WORN, AGED AND ROUGH? CAN THOSE HANDS NOT LOVE? THE ETCHED LINES IN MY HANDS SHOW THE JOURNEY THEY HAVE TAKEN FROM THE MOMENT I WAS BORN TO THIS VERY MOMENT RIGHT NOW. APPEARANCES AREN'T EVERYTHING. MY HANDS HAVE LOVED, HUGGED, CLEANED, HELD, SOOTHED, AND NURTURED. BUT YOU'LL ONLY SEE FRAIL, DAMAGED, ROUGH, BRUISED, SCARRED AND UNKEPT. EVERY HAND IS UNIQUE WITH ITS OWN STORY TO TELL. MY STORY IS YET TO BEGIN.
